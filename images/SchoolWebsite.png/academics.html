Departments and subjects

We offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to foster academic excellence and personal growth across a wide range of subjects. Our departments include:

  • School of engineering (Mathematics, french, English)
  • School of accountants (Mathematics, Economics, English)
  • School of Health sciences (Biolohy, french, English)
  • School of Building and construction (Geography, french, English)
