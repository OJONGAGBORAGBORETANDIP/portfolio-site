Discover Excellence at Phibmat University

Phibmat University (PHIBMAT), located in Bonaberi, Douala, Cameroon, is a private higher institution established in 2020. It offers undergraduate, graduate, and vocational programs across fields like business, technology, health, and engineering. PHIBMAT is committed to academic excellence and practical skills training.
